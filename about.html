Our Mission

Mental healthcare that reaches everyone who needs it.

India has one of the largest mental health treatment gaps in the world. With fewer than 0.3 psychiatrists per 100,000 people and massive geographic barriers, millions of people suffer without access to proper care.

Serenest was built to close that gap ‚Äî not by replacing in-person care, but by making high-quality psychiatric and psychological support accessible to anyone with a phone and an internet connection.

Clinical excellence, always

We never compromise on the quality of care. Every specialist is verified. Every interaction is structured for clinical outcomes.

Dignity and privacy

Mental health carries stigma in India. We've built Serenest to be a space where patients feel completely safe to seek help.

Technology in service of humanity

Every feature exists to serve patient outcomes ‚Äî not engagement metrics. We measure success in recoveries, not app opens.

The Founder

He didn't wait for someone else to build it.

Dr. Chirag Aambalia is a Consultant Psychiatrist based in Deesa, Gujarat, where he practices at Rudra Neuropsychiatry and Deaddiction Hospital. After completing his MBBS and MD in Psychiatry, he has dedicated his career to treating patients with a full spectrum of psychiatric conditions.

But years of practice revealed a pattern that troubled him deeply: patients who needed care couldn't access it ‚Äî not because qualified professionals didn't exist, but because geography, stigma, logistics, and cost created insurmountable barriers.

So he decided to build the solution himself ‚Äî teaching himself programming, design, and technology to create a platform that reflects his clinical values as much as his technical vision.

"I've seen what happens when someone doesn't get help in time. I built Serenest so that's a problem of the past, not the future."
