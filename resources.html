Quick grounding practices

5-4-3-2-1 grounding

When you feel overwhelmed, name aloud:

  • 5 things you can see
  • 4 you can touch
  • 3 you can hear
  • 2 you can smell
  • 1 you can taste

This can help bring you back to the present moment.

Box breathing

Breathe in for 4 counts, hold for 4, out for 4, hold for 4. Repeat 4–5 times. Simple and effective for calming the nervous system.
