Your onboarding journey

From application to your first patient — here’s exactly what happens when you join Serenest. We keep it simple and transparent.

1

Apply online

Day 1 · ~5 minutes

Fill out our application form with your name, contact details, specialty, years of experience, and a short introduction. No documents yet — we’ll ask for those after the first review.

What you need: Basic professional info and why you’d like to join Serenest.

We aim to acknowledge your application within 1 business day and complete an initial review within 3–5 business days.

2

Verification & review

Days 2–7

Our team verifies your credentials so we can maintain a trusted platform for patients. We’ll contact you by email (and phone if needed) to request:

  • Medical council / RCI registration details
  • Post-graduate qualification proof
  • ID and address verification (as per our policy)

You can upload documents securely via a link we send. Verification usually takes 3–5 business days once we have everything. We’ll let you know if anything is missing.

3

Agreement & platform onboarding

Days 5–10

Once verification is complete, we’ll send you:

  • Practitioner agreement — terms of engagement, fees, confidentiality, and compliance (including Indian telemedicine guidelines).
  • Platform access — login to the specialist dashboard where you’ll set up your profile and availability.

We’ll also share a short platform guide (or optional call) so you know how to manage appointments, join video sessions, and use the dashboard. Typically this step takes 1–2 days once you’ve signed and we’ve activated your account.

4

Profile setup & go live

Days 7–14

You complete your public profile: photo, bio, specialisations, languages, and consultation fees. You then set your availability (days and time slots) so patients can book you.

Once your profile is complete and approved, we go live — your profile appears on Serenest and patients can book sessions with you. You’ll receive booking notifications and can join sessions via the platform.

Ongoing: We’re here for support, platform updates, and any questions. You can update your availability and profile anytime from your dashboard.
