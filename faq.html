Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about booking, sessions, privacy, and how Serenest works.

Go to the Book a Consultation page, choose your specialist type and session type (video/audio), pick a date and time slot, and fill in your details. We’ll confirm your appointment by email. You can join the session from the link we send or from your Serenest account.
Yes. All sessions and personal data are confidential. We follow Indian data protection norms and our clinicians adhere to medical confidentiality. We do not share your information with third parties for marketing. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.
The first session is usually an assessment: the psychiatrist or therapist will ask about your concerns, history, and goals. You can ask questions and discuss what to expect from treatment. There’s no pressure — it’s a chance to see if the approach and the specialist feel right for you.
Yes. Please cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before your appointment. You can do this by replying to your confirmation email or by contacting us. Late cancellations may be subject to our cancellation policy as stated at the time of booking.
Payment is collected at or before the time of booking, depending on the option you choose. We accept major cards, UPI, and other methods as shown on the booking page. You’ll receive a receipt by email. For pricing, see our Pricing page.
Our psychiatrists can prescribe medication when clinically appropriate, in line with Indian telemedicine guidelines. Prescriptions are shared electronically and can be used at your local pharmacy. Therapists (non‑psychiatrists) do not prescribe; they provide therapy and may work alongside your psychiatrist if you’re on medication.
Our specialists are licensed to practise in India and our services are designed for patients based in India. If you’re travelling or living abroad, please contact us to check if we can support you.
