Crisis support & helplines

If you or someone you know is in crisis or having thoughts of self-harm, please reach out to one of the helplines below. They are free, confidential, and available across India.

If you are in immediate danger or someone’s life is at risk, please contact emergency services (112 or your local number) or go to the nearest hospital. Serenest is not an emergency service. For scheduled consultations, book here.

Mental health helplines (India)

Vandrevala Foundation

24/7 mental health and emotional support. Counselling and crisis intervention.

1860 2662 345 / 1800 2333 330
24 hours · All days

iCall (TISS)

Psychosocial helpline by Tata Institute of Social Sciences. Multilingual support.

022 2552 1111
Mon–Sat, 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM (IST)

Kiran – Ministry of Social Justice

Government of India mental health rehabilitation helpline.
