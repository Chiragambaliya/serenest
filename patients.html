FAQ

Questions we hear most often.

Can't find your answer? We're happy to help ‚Äî reach out anytime.

Is online psychiatry as effective as in-person treatment?

+

Yes. Multiple studies confirm that telepsychiatry is equally effective for most mental health conditions. For certain conditions requiring in-person evaluation or emergency care, your specialist will guide you accordingly. Serenest is designed to deliver the same quality of clinical care remotely.

Is my information completely confidential?

+

Absolutely. Your sessions, medical records, and personal information are encrypted and never shared without your explicit consent. We follow strict data privacy standards in line with Indian healthcare regulations. Your identity is always protected.

Can I get a prescription through Serenest?

+

Yes. Our psychiatrists can issue valid digital prescriptions where clinically appropriate, fully compliant with Indian Telemedicine Practice Guidelines (2020). However, first prescriptions for certain medications may require additional assessment steps as per guidelines.

What if I'm not sure what kind of help I need?

+

That's completely normal and more common than you think. Simply book an initial consultation and describe what you've been experiencing. Your specialist will help assess your needs, explain what's happening, and guide you to the right type of care ‚Äî whether that's psychiatry, therapy, or both.

What technology do I need for a session?

+

Just a smartphone, tablet, or computer with a camera and internet connection. You'll receive a secure link before your session ‚Äî no app downloads or complex setup required. If you have connectivity issues, we also support audio-only sessions.

How much does a session cost?

+

Session fees vary by specialist and session type. You'll see clear pricing on each specialist's profile before you book. We believe quality mental healthcare should be accessible ‚Äî our fee structure is transparent with no hidden charges.
