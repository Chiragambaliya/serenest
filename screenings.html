For Doctors
Screening tools

Check how you’ve been feeling

Short, anonymous screenings for depression, anxiety, sleep, stress, OCD, panic, and burnout. Based on widely used questionnaires. They are not a diagnosis — they help you understand whether it may be useful to talk to a professional.

These tools are for awareness only and do not replace a clinical assessment. If you are in crisis, see our Crisis support page.
🌧️

Depression

PHQ-9 based. Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by low mood, lack of interest, sleep or appetite changes, and related symptoms?

9 questions · ~2 min
😰

Anxiety

GAD-7 based. How often have you felt nervous, unable to stop worrying, or on edge over the last 2 weeks?

7 questions · ~2 min
😴

Sleep

Quick check on sleep quality, difficulty falling or staying asleep, and impact on daily life over the past 2 weeks.

6 questions · ~1 min
⚡

Stress

How stressed have you felt lately? Covers overwhelm, irritability, and coping over the past 2 weeks.

5 questions · ~1 min
🔄

OCD

Unwanted thoughts or repetitive behaviours that take time or cause distress. A short check over the past 2 weeks.

6 questions · ~2 min
💨

Panic

Sudden fear or panic attacks: racing heart, shortness of breath, dizziness. How often in the past month?

5 questions · ~1 min
🫠

Burnout

Exhaustion, emotional drain, and detachment from work or main responsibilities over the past 2 weeks.

6 questions · ~2 min
