Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025.

Serenest (“we”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and telepsychiatry services.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide when booking consultations, contacting us, or subscribing to our newsletter: name, email, phone number, and any messages or clinical information you share. We also collect technical data (e.g. IP address, browser) for security and improving our services.

How we use it

We use your information to provide consultations, send confirmations and follow-ups, respond to enquiries, send newsletter content (if you subscribed), and comply with legal and professional obligations. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Confidentiality & security

Clinical information is kept confidential and used only for your care, in line with medical ethics and applicable law. We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data where permitted by law. For requests or questions, contact us via our Contact page.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page.
