How It Works

What happens in a session.

Every session on Serenest follows a structured, clinical approach ‚Äî not just an informal chat.

01

Pre-session intake

Before your first session, you'll complete a brief intake form so your specialist can prepare and make the most of your time together.

02

Structured assessment

Your specialist conducts a thorough clinical assessment ‚Äî understanding your history, current concerns, and goals before forming any conclusions.

03

Treatment planning

Together, you'll develop a personalised care plan ‚Äî whether that involves medication, therapy, lifestyle changes, or a combination of approaches.

04

Session notes & summary

After each session, you receive a clear summary of what was discussed and agreed ‚Äî so you always know where you stand and what comes next.

05

Prescription (if applicable)

Where clinically appropriate, your psychiatrist can issue a valid digital prescription in accordance with Indian Telemedicine Practice Guidelines.

06

Ongoing follow-up

Mental health care is a journey. Regular follow-up sessions ensure your treatment is working and adjusted as your needs evolve.
