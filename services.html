What we offer

Our services

We offer support that fits how you want to grow — from self-paced tools to one-on-one guidance and group programs. Choose what works for you.

Serenest is built around three ways to engage: individual sessions for focused support, group programs for connection and shared learning, and self-guided tools for when you prefer to go at your own pace. We also support mental health professionals with resources and training for your practice.

Psychiatric conditions we support

Our services are designed to support people living with common psychiatric conditions — as a complement to clinical care, not a replacement. We focus on clarity, coping, and daily wellbeing.

  • Depression Support for low mood, motivation, and daily functioning through structured practices and connection.
  • Anxiety disorders Tools for worry, panic, and stress — including grounding, breathing, and thought patterns.
  • Stress-related conditions Burnout, adjustment difficulties, and chronic stress — practices to restore balance and focus.
  • Trauma & PTSD Gentle, trauma-informed support for safety, regulation, and reconnection (alongside professional treatment).
  • Bipolar disorder Support for stability, routine, and mood awareness in partnership with your care team.
  • OCD & related disorders Mindfulness and exposure-supportive practices that align with evidence-based treatment.

We work alongside psychiatrists, therapists, and primary care. If you’re in crisis or need urgent care, please contact a crisis line or your healthcare provider.
