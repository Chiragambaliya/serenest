Telemedicine Policy

Last updated: February 2025.

Serenest provides mental health consultations via telemedicine (video and audio) in accordance with the Telemedicine Practice Guidelines (March 2020) issued by the Board of Governors in supersession of the Medical Council of India, and other applicable laws in India.

Scope of telemedicine services

Our psychiatrists and therapists provide assessment, counselling, therapy, and (where appropriate and as per guidelines) prescription of medicines through secure video or audio consultations. The same standards of care, confidentiality, and documentation apply as in in-person practice.

When telemedicine may not be suitable

In certain situations — for example, emergency or high-risk presentation, need for physical examination, or when the clinician determines that in-person care is necessary — we may advise you to seek in-person care or emergency services. We do not provide emergency or crisis intervention through this platform; for crisis support, see our Crisis support page.

Consent and identity

By booking and attending a consultation, you consent to receive care via telemedicine. We may verify your identity at the start of a session. You are responsible for being in a private, safe environment and for the accuracy of information you provide.

Prescriptions

When a psychiatrist prescribes medication via telemedicine, prescriptions are issued in line with the Telemedicine Guidelines. Prescriptions may be shared electronically and can be used at a pharmacy of your choice. Certain controlled or restricted drugs may require additional steps as per applicable law.

Privacy and technology

We use secure platforms for video/audio consultations. Your sessions and records are confidential and handled as per our Privacy Policy. You are responsible for using a stable internet connection and a device in a private setting.

Limitations

Telemedicine has inherent limitations (e.g. no physical examination, dependence on technology). Our clinicians will exercise professional judgment to ensure safe care; when telemedicine is not appropriate, we will direct you to the right level of care.

For questions about this policy or our services, please contact us.
