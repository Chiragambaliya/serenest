We're Here For You

We're here for what you're going through.

Whatever it is — big or small — you don't have to face it alone. Our psychiatrists and therapists support a wide range of concerns.

Depression

Persistent low mood, loss of interest, and changes in sleep or appetite can be signs of depression. It's common and treatable. Our specialists offer assessment, therapy, and when appropriate, medication management.

Book a consultation

Anxiety

Excessive worry, restlessness, or panic can interfere with daily life. We help with generalised anxiety, social anxiety, panic disorder, and phobias through evidence-based therapy and support.

Book a consultation

OCD

Obsessive thoughts and compulsive behaviours can be distressing and time-consuming. Our clinicians are experienced in OCD assessment and treatment, including exposure and response prevention (ERP).

Book a consultation

PTSD

After trauma, flashbacks, nightmares, and avoidance are common. Trauma-focused therapy and support can help you process what happened and reclaim a sense of safety.

Book a consultation

Sleep Disorders

Insomnia, poor sleep quality, or sleep-related anxiety can affect your mental and physical health. We address sleep in the context of your overall wellbeing.

Book a consultation

Relationship Issues

Conflict, communication difficulties, or emotional distance in relationships can take a toll. Individual or couples-oriented support is available.

Book a consultation

Work Stress

Burnout, pressure, or work-life imbalance can impact mental health. We help you develop coping strategies and boundaries that work for you.

Book a consultation

Alcohol & Substance Use

Struggles with alcohol or substance use often need professional support. Our team can assess, counsel, and when needed, coordinate with de-addiction programmes.

Book a consultation

Bipolar Disorder

Mood swings between highs and lows need careful assessment and ongoing care. We offer diagnosis, medication management, and therapy tailored to bipolar disorder.

Book a consultation

ADHD

Attention, focus, and impulsivity challenges in adults can be assessed and supported. We provide evaluation and strategies that fit your life.

Book a consultation

Grief & Loss

Losing someone or something important can leave you overwhelmed. Grief counselling offers a space to process loss and find a path forward.

Book a consultation

Anger Management

Difficulty controlling anger can affect relationships and wellbeing. We work on understanding triggers and building healthier responses.

Book a consultation

Panic Attacks

Sudden, intense fear and physical symptoms can be frightening. Panic disorder is treatable with therapy and, when appropriate, medication.

Book a consultation

Eating Disorders

Eating disorders involve complex relationships with food, body image, and emotions. Our specialists offer assessment and support in a non-judgemental setting.

Book a consultation
